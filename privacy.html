Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Summary: LimbicEdge collects only the minimum data necessary to operate the service. We do not sell your data. We do not store your TikTok content or credentials beyond what is required for publishing.

1. Who We Are

LimbicEdge is a self-hosted content publishing application that helps creators publish short-form psychology and behavioral science videos to TikTok and YouTube. We operate as an independent application connecting to TikTok's official Content API.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data Collected from TikTok

When you connect your TikTok account via Login Kit, we access the following data through TikTok's official API:

Data TypeScope UsedPurposeRetention
Display nameuser.info.basicShow your account in our dashboardUntil disconnected
Avatar URLuser.info.basicDisplay profile image in dashboardUntil disconnected
Follower countuser.info.basicAnalytics displayUntil disconnected
Open IDuser.info.basicIdentify your account internallyUntil disconnected
Access tokenAll scopesAuthenticate API requestsEncrypted, until disconnected

We do not collect: private messages, liked videos, browsing history, location data, or any data not listed above.

2.2 Content Data

Videos you upload through LimbicEdge are:

  • Temporarily stored on our server solely for the purpose of uploading to TikTok
  • Deleted from our servers immediately after successful upload
  • Never analyzed, shared, or used for any purpose other than publishing

2.3 Application Data

  • Publishing schedule and history (stored locally, not shared)
  • Application configuration and preferences

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and maintain connection
  • Display your account information within the LimbicEdge dashboard
  • Publish videos to TikTok on your explicit instruction
  • Show publishing history and basic analytics

We never use your data for advertising, profiling, or third-party sharing.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your own server (self-hosted deployment)
  • Access tokens are stored encrypted in the local database
  • No data is transmitted to LimbicEdge servers — this is a self-hosted application
  • We recommend using HTTPS to secure your installation

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data we store about you
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data at any time
  • Revoke: Disconnect TikTok access at any time via TikTok's Security Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Export: Request a copy of your data

6. TikTok Data Deletion

To delete all TikTok-related data from LimbicEdge:

  1. Go to LimbicEdge Dashboard → Integrations
  2. Select your TikTok account → Disconnect
  3. All tokens and profile data are immediately deleted from our database

Additionally, revoke access from TikTok: Settings → Security → Connected Apps → LimbicEdge → Revoke

7. Children's Privacy

LimbicEdge is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has connected their account, contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
